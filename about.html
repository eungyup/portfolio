About Me

Passion

I'm passionate about User Experience (UX) design, web development, and digital marketing. I have worked on relevant projects at the University of Michigan and previous companies that I used to work for.


For example, As a user experience (UX) consultant, I worked for the University of Michigan College of LSA, Undergraduate Education Division last semester. I helped them redesign their Transfer Bridge Program website based on the analyzed qualitative data and an affinity wall that visualized the qualitative data. I often communicated with the project client and stakeholders during the project.


This semester, I am working for the University of Michigan Office of Student Conflict Resolution (OSCR). I will conduct a needs assessment and usability evaluation for the OSCR website and provide key findings and recommendations to the client at the end of the semester.

Volunteer - SBM Project

From 2015 through 2018, I worked on the Sending Books to Myanmar (SBM) project for educational and missionary purposes. In the end, my team members and I collected over 15,000 English books and sent them to Myanmar. For more details, please flip the image card by hovering over the image or using the tab key!

Sending Books to Myanmar (SBM) project was to send English books to public schools, public libraries, and Universities in Myanmar for educational and missional support. The core member was 6 people including me and got some volunteers occasionally.

In the end, we collected over 15,000 English books from public libraries, Universities, Salvation Army stores, Universities, and churches and sent it to Missionary Kim Gyun BAE who is a Professor at the University of Yangon and who is going to distribute the books to public schools, public libraries and Universities
